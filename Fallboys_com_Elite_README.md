# Fallboys.com --- Elite Mega-Site Blueprint (README)

## The YA Archive of Boys in Transition + The Interactive Archetype Builder

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Executive Vision

**Fallboys.com** is not "sad boy content."\
It is not toxic masculinity nostalgia.\
It is not aesthetic-only autumn branding.

It is a category-defining Young Adult literary platform centered on:

> Emotionally complex boys in transition.

Fallboys.com becomes:

-   The structured archive of male-led YA emotional coming-of-age
    stories\
-   A vulnerability-forward literary discovery engine\
-   A masculinity redefinition space\
-   A curated YA taxonomy built around transformation\
-   An interactive archetype creation platform

This is about falling --- and becoming.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1) Category Definition

## Fallboys.com

### Stories of Boys in Emotional Descent & Growth

The word "Fall" operates on two levels:

-   Falling in love, failure, grief, identity, collapse\
-   Autumn --- a season of shedding, transition, becoming

Fallboys documents the emotional architecture of adolescence for boys
and masc-aligned leads.

This platform owns:

**Male Emotional Coming-of-Age Architecture.**
